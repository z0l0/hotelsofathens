Quick answer

For a first Athens trip, start with Plaka, Monastiraki, Syntagma, or Koukaki.

Plaka is the easiest historic base, Monastiraki is best for markets and nightlife, Syntagma is the most connected luxury/business base, and Koukaki is the quieter value pick near the Acropolis.
